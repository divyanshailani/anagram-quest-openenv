🔤 Anagram Quest

Unscramble letters • Find all words • Master 5 levels

Ready to Play?

You'll receive scrambled letters and must find ALL valid English words that can be formed from them. Progress through 5 levels of increasing difficulty!

📋 Rules & Rewards

  • Find ALL anagram words — guess in any order!
  • 4 wrong guesses per level — use them wisely
  • Correct guess: +1.0 (or +0.5 after wrong guesses)
  • Right letters, not a word: +0.1
  • Wrong guess: -0.1
  • All words in level: +2.0 bonus
  • Perfect game (all 5 levels): +5.0 bonus

🏦 Banking System (Level 3+)

  • Guess correctly on 1st attempt → +1 banked chance
  • Click a failed word to spend a banked chance and recover it
  • Strategy: should you bank chances or play safe?
